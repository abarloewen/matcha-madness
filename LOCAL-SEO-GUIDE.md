## 2. Google Business Profile (biggest local‑bookings lever)

"Matcha Catering Köln" and "mobile Matcha Bar Hochzeit" are exactly what people search. A Google Business Profile puts Matcha Madness on Maps and in the local pack.

Set it up at **business.google.com** with these exact details:

- **Name:** Matcha Madness
- **Category:** Caterer (primary) · add "Event-Caterer" / "Getränke-Catering"
- **Service area:** Köln, Bonn, Düsseldorf, Leverkusen, Bergisch‑Gladbach (set as a *service‑area business* — hide the street address since you go to clients)
- **Website:** your live URL (or matchakoeln.de once pointed)
- **Phone / WhatsApp:** your booking number
- **Hours:** "Open by appointment" or your response hours
- **Photos:** upload 8–10 of the best drink + setup shots (reuse the gallery images)
- **Services:** Paket S/M/L, Hochzeits‑Catering, Henna‑Abend, Firmenevent
- **Description:** use the homepage intro text

Then: request reviews from past clients (a Google review link in your WhatsApp follow‑up works well), and post updates monthly.
